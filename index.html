Settings

Everything runs on your device. Nothing leaves it except two optional lookups: weather, and smart Q&A if you add a key below.

Voxa already handles time, date, math, timers, notes, jokes, weather, alarms, calls, and opening apps without any key.

For open, general questions, get a free key at console.groq.com/keys (no card needed) and paste it here. It's saved on this device only — you paste it once, ever.

"Open X" works for any app in Voxa's list below — it hands off to that app's own registered link, the same mechanism Alexa itself relies on. If the app isn't installed, Voxa falls back to its website. A few apps (Camera, native Gallery, system Settings) publish no such link to anyone, Alexa included, so those can't be opened by any app on any phone.

Call

Type a number, or pick straight from your contacts.

Set alarm

Rings full-screen and sends a notification while Voxa stays open in a tab — the screen will stay awake once it's counting down. This works on Android and iPhone, but the app needs to stay running (not fully closed) to go off.

Add calendar event

Opens Google Calendar pre-filled, defaulting to 1 hour from now — you confirm and save it there.
